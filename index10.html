About us

We are a company specializing in health promotion.
We offer solutions and services in: Medical tourism, E-health, Storage and distribution of future pharmaceutical products, Marketing of medical equipment, etc.



In the Congo, the majority of the population does not benefit from an adequate health service. Everyone wants to be treated outside the country, particularly in countries where medicine is more developed. But unfortunately, they can't find the opportunity to realize their healthcare plans. In fact, in the Congo, the medical evacuation system is not well organized, a situation that puts the lives of thousands of people at risk, left to their own devices for lack of information or financial resources. Many people have ventured to organize their own medical evacuations, on vague or uncertain grounds, and have ended up in hospitals with little credibility, at the hands of so-called "charlatan" doctors spending exorbitant sums. Instead of regaining their health, they lost their lives or saw their condition worsen, not knowing which saint to turn to. This is where Seven Pharma comes in.



NOS MISSIONS

Atteindre l'indice de satisfaction le plus élevé en envoyant le plus grand nombre de patients dans des hôpitaux qualifiés en Inde ou en Turquie offrant des services de soins de santé centrés sur le patient à des prix attractifs.

Devenir un partenaire facilitant l’accès à la qualité de soins de santé la plus recherchée en fournissant des services compatissants, intentionnés et de classe mondiale avec l’aide des médecins professionnels et des dernières technologies .
