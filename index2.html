WHY CHOOSE US?

We provide solutions to make life easier for our patients.

  • To become a partner facilitating access to the most sought-after quality of healthcare by providing compassionate, intentional and world-class services with the help of professional physicians and the latest technologies.

    In the Congo, the majority of the population does not benefit from an adequate health service. Everyone wants to be treated outside the country, particularly in countries where medicine is more developed. But unfortunately, they can't find the opportunity to carry out their treatment plans.
    In the Congo, the medical evacuation system is not well organized, a situation that threatens the lives of thousands of people left to their own devices for lack of information or financial resources.

    Many people have ventured to organize their own medical evacuations, on vague or uncertain grounds, and have ended up in hospitals with little credibility, at the hands of so-called "charlatan" doctors spending exorbitant sums.
